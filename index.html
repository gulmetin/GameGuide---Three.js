Game Guide

About Game

01

Game Type

The game is made in a style that combines maze and puzzle games. Maze and puzzle games are important game types in that they force the person to use their mental activities, improve their memory, and provide a holistic perspective.

Game Mods

The game has single-player and two-player game modes.

--1 Player Mod

02

1 Player Mod

The single player game mode is the game where the puzzle style comes to the fore.

The Purpose Of The Game

The aim of the game is to find the keys hidden in the dungeon rooms and reach the exit door. Keys can appear on an object, or they can appear through interaction.If the collected items are missing, the game will not be considered over. Collecting all objects is important to end the game and pass to the next level.

Design Of Dungeons

Rooms are randomly created in size and height with the codes written. Then the rooms are combined by adding necessary adjustments and corridors.

Level 01

03

Level-1

In level 1, the goal is to find the 6 keys in the rooms.

Level-1 Keys

The first key is on the table.

The second key is under the skull on the throne.

The third key is on the books on the bookshelf.

The fourth key is interactive. When approaching the chest, the chest is opened with the desired inputkey and the key is revealed.

The fifth key is on the shelf in the kitchen.

The sixth key is interactive. When approaching the last soup bowl on the dinner table, soup is drunk with the desired inputkey and the key is revealed.

Level 02

04

Level-2

In level 2, the goal is to find the 6 keys in the rooms.

Level-2 Keys

The first key is interactive.The key seen through the crystal ball cannot be retrieved unless the crystal ball is broken. When approaching the crystal ball, the desired key is entered and the crystal ball is broken. So the key can be retrieved.

The second key is interactive.When approaching the chest, the chest is opened with the desired inputkey and the key is revealed.

The third key is interactive.When approaching the fireplace, a prompt appears about adding wood. When approaching the woodshed on the side, wood is added to the fireplace with the desired inputkey. The key is revealed inside the fireplace.

The fourth key is interactive.When approaching the closed book in the throne room, a prompt about opening the book appears.When the desired key is entered, the book opens and the key comes out of the book.

The fifth key is interactive.Your eagle is trapped in a cage. When the desired key is entered, the cage is broken and the eagle is released. The key appears inside the broken cage.

The sixth key is interactive.The plants on the farm have wilted and there is no water left in the buckets. When the water is filled and returning to the farm, the plants are watered with the desired inputkey. The key is revealed on the farm.

--2 Player Mod

05

2 Player Mod

In this mode, players will have the opportunity to both have fun with each other and engage in mental activity.The game consists of different levels. The goal is to finish the maze before the other player.

Design Of Maze

The maze is created randomly according to the selected difficulty level. Different mazes appear in each game. So this mod can be played forever.

Difficulty Levels

Easy Level: Columns and rows are created by randomly choosing between 4 and 8 values.

Medium Level: Columns and rows are created by randomly selecting 9 and 12 values.

Hard Level: Columns and rows are created by randomly choosing between 13 and 15 values.
